  • Autocorrelation of the residuals

Except for the first lag, the rest of the autocorrelation coefficients are within the boundaries of negligible autocorrelation. The series might therefore still be white noise (5% of the ACF of white noise are expected to randomly exceed this threshold due to Type I error).

TO gather further evidence, a Box-Pierce or Ljung test could be carried out (not requested in this session).

  • 0 mean

The mean of the residuals differs from 0, which means that forecasts generated with this model will be somewhat biased. + More observations appear to be above 0, indicating that the model does not capture the trend.

  • Homoskedasticity

The residuals seem fairly homoskedastic, except maybe for some local heteroskedasticity aroudn 2010. The effect is not si marked to be considered relevant

  • Normality

The histogram (symmetric and with balanced tails), QQ-plot (quantiles follow the normal cummulative distribution) and Box-Plot (mean close to median and symmetry) all seem to indicate that the normality assumption is fair.
